#Team

Project Design

Guided by Prof. Pengcheng Fu and advised by Ph.D. Wei Wei, we targeted diabetes as our project topic and designed this amazing project - an in-vitro illustration of a closed loop for insulin secretion and glucose reduction. We further divided our project into several sub-projects and assigned each part to team members with passion in it.

Laboratory Work

Ruhao Sun, a Ph.D student at Hainan University, gave us insightful instructions and precious advice in all the lab work.

  • Gene Amplification & Purification:Guo Wu, Keyi Huang, and Siyi Tang
  • Plasmid Construction: Yarong Li, Shunzhi Wen, Jiahang Du, and Sicun Han
  • Preparation of Competent Cells & Transformation: En Lei, Shunzhi Wen
    Students mainly did all the experiment including PCR, establishment of bacteria, and data analyzing under the instruction of Ph.D. Ruhao Sun.

Organ-on-a-chip:

Disheng Yang: introduces organ-on-a-chip based on microfluid and its application on Biomedical industry; the introduction provides the idea of designing of organ-on-a-chip in our project.

Guo Wu, Siyi Tang, Yarong Li, and Shunzhi Wen: chip design
En Lei, Sicun Han, and Siyi Tang: chip fabrication
Shunzhi Wen, Qingyue Gao, Mingyuan Li, and Kehan Zhan: liver cell cultivation and fluorescence image analysis
Keyi Huang, Mingyuan Li, and Kehan Zhan: combination of the chip with sensors

Electrochemical Sensor:

Yinqiang Wu: helps with the design of the enzyme electrochemical sensor used in our project, which lays foundation for the following experiment.

En Lei, Shunzhi Wen, Jiahang Du, and Sicun Han: sensor design
Qingyue Gao, Shunzhi Wen, and Kehan Zhan: sensor model and stimulation
Guo Wu, En Lei, and Qingyue Gao: sensor fabrication and characterization

Electric Circuit:

Wenyan Zhu supports our team with directing all members using the devices and attributes a lot at the period of troubleshooting.

Guo Wu, En Lei, Yarong Li, and Shunzhi Wen: electric circuit design

Jiahang Du, Qingyue Gao, and Shunzhi Wen: electric circuit fabrication

Shunzhi Wen, Jiahang Du, and Sicun Han: application of electric circuit
